Our Mission

We believe security investigations should be comprehensive, factual, and accessible. Diverg was founded to bridge the gap between traditional web security and blockchain investigation—providing a single platform that sees the full picture.

Whether you're tracking a sophisticated attacker across multiple chains, assessing a token launch for investment, or conducting authorized penetration testing, Diverg delivers the tools you need with data you can trust.

What Sets Us Apart

Fact-Only Data

We never present placeholder data as real. When APIs are configured, you get live data; when they're not, reports clearly indicate what's missing.

Multi-Surface

Web, blockchain, and OSINT in one unified investigation. No more switching between ten different tools to understand a target.

Investigator-First

Built by security professionals who understand the workflow. From Telegram delivery for quick checks to detailed flow diagrams for deep analysis.

Transparent

Clear documentation, honest reporting, and visible methodology. We believe in showing our work.

Technology

Diverg is built on a modular skill-based architecture powered by modern LLM reasoning. Our agents follow established methodologies (OWASP Testing Guide, PTES) while adapting dynamically to target types.

We integrate with industry-standard data sources—Solscan Pro, Arkham, Etherscan, Bubblemaps—while maintaining the flexibility to work standalone when API keys aren't available.
